MEMBER SUPPORT · THE SECRETARY'S OFFICE

The Club secretary is available to assist members with matters of a technical or administrative nature. We ask that all enquiries be conducted with the composure befitting a member of your standing.

I. The Daily Directive

A new directive is issued to all members simultaneously at midnight GMT. If today's directive has not yet appeared, we recommend closing and reopening the app. The archives are occasionally slow to warm.

Directives are the same for all members globally. They cannot be changed, skipped, or negotiated.

II. Verified Sightings

Evidence submitted to Verified Sightings must relate to the current day's directive. Submissions are reviewed by fellow members, whose verdicts are final and beyond appeal.

To remove a submission, navigate to your Dossier, locate the relevant entry, and withdraw it at your discretion. Removed submissions leave no record.

IV. Your Alias

Your alias is assigned upon entry by the Club. Should you find it unsuitable, a single amendment may be requested. However, once a new alias is accepted and sealed, it is binding for a period of thirty days. No further changes will be entertained during this time.

Choose carefully. The Club is watching.

V. Prestige & The Ledger

A member's standing is measured in Sovereigns (§) — the Club's sole unit of distinction. Net Worth is accrued through verdicts received on Verified Sightings.

Distinguished verdict — §200

Acceptable verdict — §100

Unrefined verdict — §0

VI. Payments & Refunds

Bullion is available for a one-time upfront purchase via the Apple App Store. The price varies by country and region. There are no in-app purchases. There are no subscriptions. No further payment will ever be requested.

All payment queries, including refund requests, must be directed to Apple directly via reportaproblem.apple.com. The Club does not process payments and cannot issue refunds.

VII. Technical Difficulties

Should you encounter a matter of Temporary Market Instability — that is to say, a technical error — we recommend the following in order:

Close and reopen the app. Ensure your device is running the latest version of Bullion. Check your internet connection. If the matter persists, contact the secretary below.

VIII. Conduct & Reporting

The Club expects its members to conduct themselves with dignity at all times. Should you wish to Lodge a Formal Complaint regarding another member's conduct, please contact the secretary with the relevant details. All complaints are handled with discretion and appropriate gravity.

— A WORD OF CAUTION —

By taking on the directives set forth within this chamber, you acknowledge, upon your own judgment and at your own risk, that any challenge, spectacle, or act undertaken shall be done solely of your own free will and discretion. Let it be clearly understood that neither this Hall, nor its Committee, nor any Keeper, Steward, or affiliated soul advises, commands, encourages, or condones any act unlawful, harmful, obscene, reckless, or offensive to the dignity and peace of others.

All pursuits undertaken herein are expected to remain within the bounds of law, civility, and sound reason. Should a member choose to stray beyond such bounds, the burden, consequence, and accountability thereof shall rest upon them alone.

Proceed, then, with wit tempered by wisdom, courage governed by restraint, and honor kept intact.

IX. Exiting the Club

Should you wish to Exit the Club, you may delete the app from your device at any time. Deleting the app removes it from your device only. Your submissions to Verified Sightings will remain in the archive until explicitly withdrawn.

To erase your record entirely, navigate to your Dossier, select Personal Archive, and withdraw all entries prior to deleting the app. Withdrawn submissions leave no record. Your alias will be returned to the pool and your dossier sealed.
