MEMBER SUPPORT · THE SECRETARY'S OFFICE

The Club secretary is available to assist members with matters of a technical or administrative nature. We ask that all enquiries be conducted with the composure befitting a member of your standing.

I. The Daily Directive

A new directive is issued to all members simultaneously each day. If today's directive has not yet appeared, we recommend closing and reopening the app. The archives are occasionally slow to warm.

Directives are the same for all members globally. They cannot be changed, skipped, or negotiated.

II. Verified Sightings

Evidence submitted to Verified Sightings must relate to the current day's directive. Submissions are reviewed by fellow members, whose verdicts determine a member's standing within the Club.

To remove a submission, navigate to your Dossier, select Personal Archive, and withdraw the relevant entry. Withdrawn submissions are removed from active circulation within the Club on a best-effort basis. Please note that verdicts cast by other members on a withdrawn sighting, and any reports filed against it, may remain as part of the Club's moderation record. These are not surfaced publicly.

III. Camera & Photo Access

Bullion requests access to your device camera and photo library solely for the submission of evidence to Verified Sightings. No access is used for advertising, tracking, or any unrelated purpose whatsoever. You may revoke this access at any time via your device settings. The Club will note your discretion.

IV. Correspondence & Chambers

Direct correspondence requires a mutual Introduction before Dispatches may be exchanged. If an Introduction has been requested and not yet accepted, the matter is in the other party's hands. Patience is a virtue the Club holds in high regard.

Chambers may be created by any member and are protected by a wax seal password of the founder's choosing. If you have misplaced a Chamber password, it cannot be recovered. The Club does not hold spare keys.

V. Your Alias

Your alias is assigned upon entry by the Club. Should you find it unsuitable, a single amendment may be requested. However, once a new alias is accepted and sealed, it is binding for a period of thirty days. No further changes will be entertained during this time.

Choose carefully. The Club is watching.

VI. Prestige & The Ledger

A member's standing is measured in Sovereigns (§) — the Club's sole unit of distinction. Net Worth is accrued through verdicts received on Verified Sightings.

Distinguished verdict — §200

Acceptable verdict — §100

Recently Rich verdict — §0

The Ledger is a live ranking of all members by current Net Worth. It updates in real time as verdicts are issued.

VII. Payments

Bullion is currently available as a web application. No payment is required at this time. Should a paid iOS application become available via the Apple App Store in the future, this section will be updated accordingly. There are no in-app purchases. There are no subscriptions. No payment will ever be requested without prior notice.

VIII. Your Data

When you sign in via Apple, your email address and a unique identifier are stored securely as part of your account record. Your alias, prestige tier, induction date, verdicts cast, achievements earned, and any content you submit are all associated with your account.

Authentication and access logs — including IP addresses and device information — are maintained by the Club's hosting infrastructure for operational and security purposes only. No third-party advertising or analytics tracking is employed.

Your data is not sold. It is not shared with advertisers. It is not used for any purpose beyond operating the Club.

To request full deletion of your account and all associated personal data, contact the Club secretary directly at the address below.

IX. Technical Difficulties

Should you encounter a matter of Temporary Market Instability — that is to say, a technical error — we recommend the following in order:

Close and reopen the app. Ensure your device is running the latest version of Bullion. Check your internet connection. If the matter persists, contact the secretary below with a brief description of the issue and the device you are using.

X. Conduct & Reporting

The Club expects its members to conduct themselves with dignity at all times. Should you wish to Lodge a Formal Complaint regarding another member's conduct, please contact the secretary with the relevant details. All complaints are handled with discretion and appropriate gravity.

— A WORD OF CAUTION —

By taking on the directives set forth, you acknowledge, upon your own judgment and at your own risk, that any challenge, spectacle, or act undertaken shall be done solely of your own free will and discretion. Let it be clearly understood that neither this Hall, nor its Committee, nor any Keeper, Steward, or affiliated soul advises, commands, encourages, or condones any act unlawful, harmful, obscene, reckless, or offensive to the dignity and peace of others.

All pursuits undertaken herein are expected to remain within the bounds of law, civility, and sound reason. Should a member choose to stray beyond such bounds, the burden, consequence, and accountability thereof shall rest upon them alone.

XI. Exiting the Club

Deleting the app removes it from your device only. Your account record — including your email address, unique identifier, alias, prestige standing, verdicts, and achievements — will remain in the Club's archive.

To remove your uploaded sightings, navigate to your Dossier, select Personal Archive, and withdraw all entries before deleting the app. Please note this does not erase your full account record.

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated personal data, contact the Club secretary directly. We will action your request with appropriate gravity.

XII. Contact the Secretary

For matters not addressed above, the Club secretary may be reached at:

We endeavour to respond within two business days. Matters of genuine urgency will be treated accordingly.
