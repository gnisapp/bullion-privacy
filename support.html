MEMBER SUPPORT · THE SECRETARY'S OFFICE

The Club secretary is available to assist members with matters of a technical or administrative nature. We ask that all enquiries be conducted with the composure befitting a member of your standing.

I. The Daily Directive

A new directive is issued to all members simultaneously at midnight GMT. If today's directive has not yet appeared, we recommend closing and reopening the app. The archives are occasionally slow to warm.

Directives are identical for all members globally. They cannot be skipped, altered, or negotiated.

II. Verified Sightings

Evidence submitted to Verified Sightings must relate to the current directive. Submissions are reviewed by fellow members, whose verdicts contribute to a member's standing within the Club.

To remove a submission, navigate to your Dossier and withdraw the relevant entry from your Personal Archive. Withdrawn submissions are removed from active systems within a reasonable period of time.

Please note that moderation records, reports, or verdict metadata associated with withdrawn submissions may remain internally for security and operational purposes. Such records are never displayed publicly.

III. Camera & Photo Access

Bullion requests access to your camera and photo library solely to facilitate the submission of evidence to Verified Sightings.

The Club does not use this access for advertising, tracking, analytics profiling, or any unrelated purpose whatsoever. Access permissions may be revoked at any time through your device settings.

IV. Your Alias

Your alias is assigned upon entry by the Club. Should you find it unsuitable, an amendment may be requested.

Once accepted and sealed, an alias amendment becomes binding for a period of thirty days. No further changes may be entertained during this interval.

Choose carefully. The Club is watching.

V. Prestige & The Ledger

A member's standing is measured in Sovereigns (§) — the Club's sole unit of distinction. Net Worth is accrued through verdicts received on Verified Sightings.

DISTINGUISHED VERDICT — §200

ACCEPTABLE VERDICT — §100

UNREFINED VERDICT — §0

The Ledger reflects the current standing of all members and updates continuously as verdicts are issued.

VI. Payments & Purchases

Bullion is available as a one-time upfront purchase via the Apple App Store. Pricing may vary by region according to Apple's local storefront policies.

All payments are processed entirely by Apple. The Club does not receive or store payment card information of any kind.

There are no subscriptions. There are no recurring fees. No further payment will ever be requested within the Club.

Refund requests must be directed to Apple through their official support systems.

VII. Your Data

When signing in via Apple, your email address and account identifier are securely associated with your membership record.

Your alias, prestige standing, verdict history, achievements, uploaded sightings, and moderation history are retained as part of the operation of the Club.

Authentication logs, IP addresses, and device-related access information may also be retained for operational integrity, abuse prevention, and security monitoring.

The Club does not sell personal data. It does not share personal data with advertisers. It does not employ third-party advertising networks or behavioural tracking technologies.

To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, contact the secretary directly at the address below.

VIII. Technical Difficulties

Should you encounter a matter of Temporary Market Instability — that is to say, a technical error — we recommend the following in order:

Close and reopen the app. Ensure your device is running the latest version of Bullion. Confirm that your internet connection remains stable.

If the matter persists, contact the secretary with a concise description of the issue and the device currently in use.

IX. Conduct & Reporting

The Club expects all members to conduct themselves with dignity befitting the chamber.

Should you wish to lodge a formal complaint regarding another member's conduct, contact the secretary with the relevant details. All reports are reviewed discreetly and with appropriate gravity.

— A WORD OF CAUTION —

By undertaking the directives issued within this chamber, you acknowledge that all acts, challenges, spectacles, and pursuits are performed solely at your own discretion and entirely at your own risk.

Neither the Club, nor its Committee, nor any Keeper, Steward, or affiliated party advises, commands, encourages, or condones unlawful, dangerous, harmful, obscene, reckless, or offensive conduct.

All pursuits undertaken within Bullion are expected to remain within the bounds of law, civility, and sound judgment. Responsibility for actions taken beyond those bounds rests exclusively with the member concerned.

X. Exiting the Club

Deleting the app removes it from your device only. Your account record and associated membership data may remain within the Club's infrastructure unless separately deleted.

Uploaded sightings may be withdrawn individually through your Dossier prior to account deletion.

To request complete erasure of your membership record and associated personal data, contact the secretary directly.
