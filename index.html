PRIVACY POLICY · IN CONFIDENCE · IN PERPETUITY

EST. 2025 · LAST UPDATED: MAY 2026

The Club takes the matter of discretion with the same gravity it applies to all things. What follows is a frank and complete account of what is collected, how it is used, and what remains entirely your own.

I. What We Collect

Bullion collects a limited and specific set of data necessary to operate the Club. The following is held on your behalf from the moment you enter.

EMAIL ADDRESS — When you sign in via Apple, your email address is written to your account record and held by the Club's backend infrastructure. It is not displayed within the app, but the Club is the data controller for this information.

USER IDENTIFIER — A unique identifier (UUID) is created at sign-in and attached to every action taken within the Club — sightings submitted, verdicts cast, reports filed, appeals made, achievements earned, and roles held.

AUTHENTICATION & ACCESS LOGS — The Club's backend records sign-in events, IP addresses, and device information by default as part of standard infrastructure operations. These logs are maintained for security and operational purposes.

MEMBER RECORD — Your assigned alias, prestige tier, induction date, Net Worth in Sovereigns, completed directives, verdicts cast, reports filed, appeals made, and achievements earned are all stored and associated with your account.

IMAGE & VIDEO UPLOADS — Evidence submitted to Verified Sightings is stored securely and associated with your account identifier. It is displayed within the Club to fellow members for the purpose of receiving verdicts.

CORRESPONDENCE — Dispatches exchanged between members and Chamber conversations are stored securely and accessible only to the parties involved.

II. How We Use Your Data

Data collected by Bullion is used for one purpose only — to operate the app and deliver the Club experience to its members. It is not used for advertising. It is not sold. It is not shared with third parties for commercial purposes. It is not used to build profiles beyond what is necessary to maintain your membership record.

Server-side request logs and error records are maintained by the Club's hosting infrastructure for operational and security purposes only. Bullion does not use advertising trackers or sell personal data to advertisers..

III. Verified Sightings

Uploads submitted to Verified Sightings may be withdrawn at any time at your sole discretion via your Dossier. Upon withdrawal, the submission and its associated file are removed from active systems within a reasonable period of time. However, verdicts cast by other members on a withdrawn sighting, and any reports filed against it, may remain as part of the Club's moderation record. These are not surfaced publicly.

IV. Camera & Photo Library

Bullion requests access to your camera and photo library solely to facilitate the submission of evidence to Verified Sightings. This access is used for no other purpose whatsoever. You may revoke it at any time via your device settings. The Club will note your discretion.

V. Payments

Bullion is currently available as a web application. Should a paid iOS application be made available via the Apple App Store in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly to reflect any payment processing arrangements. No payment data is collected by the Club at this time.

VI. What We Do Not Do

The Club does not sell your data. It does not share your data with advertisers. It does not employ third-party advertising or analytics tracking technologies. Standard server-side infrastructure logs are maintained by the Club's hosting provider for operational purposes, as is common practice for all hosted applications.

We find gratuitous data collection beneath us.

VII. Third Party Services

APPLE SIGN IN — Authentication is handled by Apple via OAuth. Apple processes your sign-in and may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at apple.com/privacy.

SUPABASE / LOVABLE CLOUD — The Club's database, file storage, and hosting infrastructure is provided by Supabase and Lovable Cloud. All member data — including account records, uploads, and correspondence — is stored on their infrastructure on the Club's behalf. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

VIII. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data held about you. To exercise any such right, contact the Club secretary at the address below. We will respond with the promptness the matter deserves.

IX. Data Retention

Your account record — including your email address, user identifier, alias, prestige standing, verdicts, reports, appeals, and achievements — is retained for as long as your account exists within the Club's infrastructure.

Withdrawing your uploaded sightings via your Dossier removes those files from active systems within a reasonable period of time. However, your core account record, authentication data, and access logs are not removed by this action alone. Signing in again with the same Apple credentials will restore full access to your existing account.

To request full deletion of your account and associated personal data, contact the Club secretary directly. We will action your request with appropriate gravity.

X. Children

Bullion is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. The Club does not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has accessed the Club, please contact the secretary below.

XI. Changes to This Policy

Should this policy require amendment, the updated version will be published here with the date of revision noted. Continued use of the app following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

XII. Contact

Matters of a private nature may be directed to the Club secretary at:

privacy@bullionapp.com

We respond to all legitimate enquiries with the promptness the matter deserves.
